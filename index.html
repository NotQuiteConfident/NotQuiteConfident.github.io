Expeditor | Twist and Turn Tavern | 2015 - 2016

An Expeditor works to ensure the efficient flow of food from a kitchen to the wait staff. They assist in the assembly of a plate, though not always the cooking, as well as help bring food to wait staff or directly to customers. They are generally back of house staff.

The Expeditor is responsible for ensuring that every plate is correct to what is ordered and assembling all the plates into an order. They may also be responsible for running some orders and assisting the cooking staff.

Student Police Aide | University of Maryland Police Department | 2016 - Present

A Student Police Aide works as part of the University of Maryland Police Auxiliary to assist the UMPD in creating a safe campus community. They will often help manage traffic, undertake patrols of the campus, assit in special events or guard univesity buildings and construction sites.

An SPA is responsible for assiting in keeping the campus safe and ensuring that they conduct themselves in a way that is in keeping with the public trust.

Flight Instructor | Freeway Airport | Never - I Wish

A Flight Instructor assists in the training of pilots. They will educate student pilots of the various aspects of flight, both those of actual flight and the various legal responsiblities. They will fly with students to train and measure their flying skills. They will also quiz a student on the non flying aspects of being a pilot.

A Flight Instructor is responsible for ensuring that their students are competent before advancing them to the next stage of their training. They are also responsible for signing off on a student pilot's various qualifications. Additonally, they must judge when a student is ready to attempt a check ride to get their license.
